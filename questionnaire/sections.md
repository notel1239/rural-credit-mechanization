# Questionnaire Sections

This file outlines the four core sections of the farmer questionnaire. Each section corresponds to a GitHub Issue that will be created for detailed discussion.

## Section A: Household & Farm Demographics
- Age of household head
- Education level of household head
- Household size
- Total cultivable land owned/operated (hectares)
- Main crop(s) grown
- Access to irrigation (yes/no)
- Years of farming experience
- Presence of off‑farm income sources (yes/no)

## Section B: Formal Credit Access
- Have you ever applied for a bank loan for agricultural purposes? (yes/no)
- If yes, was the loan approved? (yes/no)
- Amount of loan received (if approved)
- Interest rate (if known)
- Collateral required (yes/no)
- Have you used any government credit schemes? (yes/no)
- Satisfaction with formal credit process (scale 1‑5)

## Section C: Informal Credit Sources
- Have you borrowed from money lenders in the last 3 years? (yes/no)
- Have you obtained inputs (seeds, fertilizer) on credit from suppliers? (yes/no)
- Are you a member of a rotating savings group (ROSCA)? (yes/no)
- Amount typically borrowed from informal sources
- Interest rate (if any)
- Reasons for preferring informal credit (multiple choice)

## Section D: Machinery & Input Decisions
- Did you purchase or lease a tractor/tractor‑trolley in the last 3 years? (yes/no) – **dependent variable**
- If yes, source of financing (own savings, formal loan, informal loan, both)
- Type of machinery acquired
- Cost of machinery
- Perceived impact on productivity (scale 1‑5)
- Future intention to acquire more machinery (yes/no)
